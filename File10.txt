Zubair ibn al-Awam (R.A)<br>

Date of Birth: 594 CE<br>
Date of Death: 656 CE<br>
City of Residence: Medina, Saudi Arabia<br>
Place of Birth: Makkah, Saudi Arabia<br>
<br>
Zubair ibn al-Awam, renowned for his bravery and unwavering commitment to Islam, played a significant role in the early 
Islamic conquests. His participation in pivotal battles such as Badr and Uhud, alongside his notable military 
contributions, established him as a respected and esteemed figure among the companions of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).
 Recognized as one of the Ten Promised Paradise, Zubair's dedication to the principles of justice and righteousness 
 remains an exemplary model for Muslims striving to uphold the values of courage and faith.
