Abdur Rahman ibn Awf (R.A)<br>

Date of Birth: 581 CE<br>
Date of Death: 652 CE<br>
City of Residence: Medina, Saudi Arabia<br>
Place of Birth: Makkah, Saudi Arabia<br>
<br>
Abdur Rahman ibn Awf, recognized for his exceptional business acumen and philanthropy, contributed significantly to the 
welfare of the early Muslim community. His proficiency in trade and commerce enabled him to play a crucial role in the 
distribution of the spoils of war during the early Islamic conquests. Renowned for his generosity and kindness, Abdur 
Rahman's charitable endeavors and contributions to the growth of the Muslim community exemplify the importance of 
economic empowerment and social welfare in Islam.
