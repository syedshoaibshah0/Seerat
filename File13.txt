Sa'id ibn Zayd (R.A) <br>

Date of Birth: 	c. 593–594 CE
 <br>
Date of Death: c. 671 (aged 77–78) <br>
City of Residence: Not Available <br>
Place of Birth: Mecca, Hejaz, Arabia <br> 
<br>
Sa'id ibn Zayd, recognized for his righteousness and piety, was a close companion of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). 
Though specific details about his life are not widely documented, his unwavering dedication to the teachings of Islam 
and his active participation in various early Islamic battles exemplify his commitment to upholding the principles of 
faith and justice.
