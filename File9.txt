Talha ibn Ubayd-Allah (R.A)<br>

Date of Birth: 596 CE<br>
Date of Death: 656 CE<br>
City of Residence: Medina, Saudi Arabia<br>
Place of Birth: Makkah, Saudi Arabia<br>
<br>
 Talha ibn Ubayd-Allah, recognized for his role in various early Islamic battles, demonstrated unwavering dedication to 
 the cause of Islam. His participation in the Battle of Uhud and other significant military campaigns solidified his 
 reputation as a valiant and courageous companion of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Notably counted among the Ten Promised 
 Paradise, Talha's loyalty and devotion to the teachings of Islam exemplify the virtues of sacrifice and bravery for the 
 sake of upholding the faith.
