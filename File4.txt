Ashra e Mubashsharah (R.A): "Ashra Mubashshara" refers to the ten companions of the Islamic prophet Muhammad (PBUH) who 
were promised paradise during their lifetimes by the Prophet himself. The list includes the following individuals
<br>
1.  Hazrat Ayesha (R.A)<br>
2.  Hazrat Khadija (R.A)<br>
3.  Abu Bakr (R.A)<br>
4.  Umar ibn al-Khattab (R.A)<br>
5.  Usman ibn e Affan (R.A)<br>
6.  Ali ibn e Abi Talib (R.A)<br>
7.  Talha ibn e Ubayd-Allah (R.A)<br>
8.  Zubayr ibn al-Awam (R.A)<br>
9.  Abdur Rahman ibn Awf (R.A)<br>
10.  Sa'd ibn Abi Waqqas (R.A)<br>
11.  Sa'id ibn Zayd (R.A)<br>
12. Abu Ubaidah ibn al-Jarrah (R.A)<br>
